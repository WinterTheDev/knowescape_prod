Our Founder

Norman is a qualified Training and Development Manager and Enterprise and Supplier Development specialist with over 20 years' experience in Training, Enterprise Development and Mentoring. He is passionate about training and development and is committed to excellence in customer service. It is this drive that led to the formation of Knowescape Consulting. He is also overseeing the overall quality assurance across all our offerings. Norman has worked with SMMEs conducting training, coaching and mentoring for various organisations. He has managed and overseen Skills and ESD projects on behalf of multinational clients over the years. He holds a bachelor's degree in business administration, majoring in Entrepreneurship and Innovation.

His Vision

Norman strives to ensure that only people considered experts in their respective fields form part of the Knowescape Consulting team. In this way, you can rest secure in the knowledge your organisation and your beneficiaries are receiving the best service delivered in the most professional, understandable manner possible.
